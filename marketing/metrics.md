# Metrics: the only numbers that matter

Stems is not a high-volume funnel. It's a relationship-driven tool for a small,
specific group of people. Vanity metrics (impressions, followers) will lie to us.
Track the things that mean a grower is actually getting value.

## The one top-line number

**Activated growers:** growers who have (a) set up a page, (b) listed at least
one flower with a photo, and (c) shared their link at least once (link in bio, a
post, a market sign). Not signups. Not visits. Growers actually using it.

> V1 goal: **10 activated growers** genuinely using Stems and telling other
> growers. That's the whole game. Everything below feeds that.

## Supporting metrics (in priority order)

1. **Activated growers** (above). Count manually in V1; it's a small enough
   number to know each one by name.
2. **Pages shared / links in bios.** How many growers have actually put their
   `stems.market/@handle` somewhere public. Check by visiting their Instagram.
3. **Availability freshness.** Of activated growers, how many updated in the last
   2 weeks. A page nobody updates is a page nobody trusts. This is the real
   retention signal.
4. **Buyer-side interest per grower.** Hard to measure directly in V1 (contact is
   off-platform, by design). Proxy: page views on `/@handle`, and asking growers
   "are you getting enquiries through it?". Qualitative beats a dashboard here.
5. **Grower-to-grower referrals.** New growers who say another grower told them.
   This is the flywheel; track the source on every new grower.
6. **Discovery searches.** Searches on `/discover` and what people search for
   (tells us what buyers want and which areas have demand but no growers).

## How we capture it (V1, mostly manual)

- A simple **grower log** (spreadsheet or Notion): name, handle, joined date,
  listed-flowers? shared-link? last-updated, "how did you find us", enquiries
  reported. `TODO(jamie): set this up.`
- **"How did you find us?"** asked in every founder welcome / outreach email
  (see `../05-email/`). The single most valuable question we can ask.
- **Analytics:** not yet wired (no GA4/analytics in the app currently, despite a
  README mention). Tracked in `../seo-roadmap/`. Until then, lean on the manual
  log and direct conversation. For 10 growers, conversation is better data anyway.

## What we deliberately do NOT optimise for

- Follower counts on the Stems socials.
- Total signups (a signup who never lists or shares is not a win).
- Engagement-rate vanity (likes, comments) as a goal in itself.
- Anything that would push us to make growers feel like a number.

## Review cadence

Once a fortnight, update the grower log and answer one question: **are the
growers we have getting value, and would they tell another grower?** If yes,
get more growers. If no, fix that first. Don't pour distribution into a leaky
experience.

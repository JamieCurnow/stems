The plan to get Stems its first real growers, and the buyers those growers bring
with them. Honest, relationship-driven, unhurried, the opposite of a growth-hack
playbook. Read `00-foundations/positioning.md` before anything else.

## The honest read on this product

1. The hero is the **grower**, not the buyer. We acquire growers. Florists and
   event buyers come because a grower they already know sent them a link.
2. Stems is the **shopfront for a small grower**, the Linktree/Bandcamp for
   someone who grows flowers in a field. We make them look legitimate and
   findable without making them build a website.
3. So we're not selling a marketplace. We're giving a grower a calm, good-looking
   place to show what they've grown, that saves them retyping their availability
   into a dozen chats.
4. This is a small, relationship-driven world (British-grown, slow flowers,
   Flowers from the Farm). Every move respects that. If a tactic would make a
   grower feel commodified or sold to, it's wrong, even if it would "convert".

## Target audience (in priority order)

1. **Small flower growers.** The people we acquire. Already on Instagram, proud
   of the craft, time-poor in the season. Highest priority, every channel serves
   reaching them.
2. **Other growers in the same scene.** Grower-to-grower word of mouth is the
   flywheel. A grower's page is our best advert to the next grower.
3. **Local & event florists.** The demand a grower brings. Also reachable
   directly over time via SEO and content.
4. **Conscious local buyers / the public.** Want British, seasonal, named
   flowers. Long-tail SEO and the buyer-side story.

## The flywheel

```
A grower sets up a Stems page  (the one thing we must make happen)
        │
        ▼
They put the @handle link at the top of their Instagram and in their bio
        │
        ▼
Existing followers & florist contacts click it for this week's stock
        │
        ├──► some are growers too ("how'd you make that page?") ──► grower-to-grower word of mouth
        │
        ▼
Buyers searching "local flowers near me" find growers on /discover (SEO + AEO)
        │
        ▼
More growers join because their peers are on it ──► loop reseeds
```

The multiplier is **grower-to-grower**: the people most likely to admire a
grower's setup are other growers in the same county group and market. That's why
`02-grower-outreach` is the most important channel.

## Channels and effort split

| Channel | Effort | Priority | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Grower outreach** (`02`) | High | 1 | The acquisition engine. Find and warmly bring on the first 10-20 growers, one by one, through Flowers from the Farm, markets, local groups. Nothing matters more. |
| **Instagram** (`01`) | High | 1 | Where growers already are, and where their Stems link lives. Feature growers, model what a good page looks like, reach the next grower. |
| **SEO / content** (`03`) | Medium | 2 | Slow, compounds. Grower-facing posts double as acquisition; buyer-facing posts bring demand. See `03-seo-content/pillar-articles.md`. |
| **AEO / GEO** (`07`) | Medium | 2 | Near-zero competition in this niche. Get recommended when people ask AI "how do I sell flowers locally" / "where to buy british flowers". Compounds like SEO. |
| **Word of mouth** (`04`) | Low | 3 | Manual in V1 (no referral feature). Featuring growers so they share, asking happy growers to tell their group. |
| **Email** (`05`) | Low | 3 | Welcome growers, nudge first listing + share, seasonal check-ins. Infra (Resend) already built. Cheap and warm. |
| **Community & press** (`06`) | Medium | 4 | Local press, gardening/floristry press, slow-flowers podcasts, FFTF partnerships. Founder-led, warm PR. |

## Writing voice (do not break)

1. No em-dashes. Anywhere. Ever.
2. Warm, calm, honest, unhurried. Never hype, corporate, or salesy.
3. No flower puns. Ever.
4. Celebrate the grower, never patronise them.
5. The story can be romantic about flowers; the product stays a plain, honest
   shopfront. We never promise sales, audiences, or success.
6. Make the positive case for local/British/seasonal, never the guilt case.

Full detail: `00-foundations/brand-voice.md` and `social-voice.md`.

## First 30 days (skim)

**Week 1, foundations.** Voice locked (done). Bios + handles set. Instagram
account live with 3-5 starter posts. The 5 most wantable local growers
identified for outreach. The technical SEO quick wins (sitemap, robots, global
schema) shipped (see `seo-roadmap/`).

**Week 2, first growers.** Reach out to 5 growers, warmly, one at a time
(`02-grower-outreach`). Offer to set their page up with them. Goal: 3 live pages
with links in bios.

**Week 3, content + presence.** First blog post published (fact-checked). Active
in one Flowers from the Farm / grower group, genuinely. Feature the first growers
on Instagram so they share.

**Week 4, review.** Are the growers we have getting value and telling others?
(See `metrics.md`.) If yes, get more. If no, fix the experience first.
